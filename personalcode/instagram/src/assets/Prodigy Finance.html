FAQ

What will happen after accepting the offer?

You will need to upload documents to verify the information you provided in your application.

Can I change the loan amount after accepting the provisional offer?

Yes, you can change the loan amount or withdraw your application entirely by contacting our customer service team.

When can I get a loan confirmation letter for visa purposes?

Once your documents are verified, we will send your loan confirmation letter.

How will I receive the money?

The money will be sent directly to your school. If the loan covers living expenses, your school will give you that money.

When can I start repaying back my loan?

You only have to start repaying your loan 6 months after you finish classes, but you can start repaying it as soon as the loan is disbursed.
